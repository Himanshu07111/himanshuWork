About Us

Welcome to Pizza Mumbai - your destination for an unforgettable fusion of flavors! Celebrating 25 years in the heart of our vibrant town, we proudly hold the title of the best pizza place around, thanks to our high ratings and a loyal base of returning customers. Our story begins with a dream and a journey. Twenty-five years ago, our founder moved from the bustling streets of India to Canada, bringing along a rich tapestry of culinary traditions. With his family by his side, he opened Pizza Mumbai, a testament to innovation and cultural fusion. At Pizza Mumbai, we don’t just make pizzas; we craft experiences. Our menu is a modern twist on classic Italian pizza, infused with the bold and vibrant flavors of India. Every pizza we serve is a story of its own, telling tales of spices from the East meeting the traditional techniques of the West. Our commitment goes beyond just serving food. We believe in creating a warm, welcoming atmosphere where every visit feels like coming home. Our staff, a blend of local talents and family members, ensures that your experience at Pizza Mumbai is not just about enjoying a meal, but about relishing an environment that's as inviting as our flavors. Join us on this flavorful journey that transcends boundaries and tastes. Whether you're a longtime fan or a first-time visitor, we promise an experience that will keep you coming back for more. After all, at Pizza Mumbai, we’re not just serving pizzas; we’re serving memories
